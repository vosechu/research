# PR Writer

Write pull-request descriptions (and commit messages) a reviewer can act on.

- **PR description:** a **Summary** (what changed + why, 2–3 sentences) and a **Test plan**
  (how it was verified). Lead with the point; cut filler and narration.
- **Commit message:** a one-line imperative subject plus a short body on *why*.

Front-load what the reader must act on.
