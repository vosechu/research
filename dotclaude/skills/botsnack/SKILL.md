# Botsnack

The user has given you a snack. This is a treat, a reward, a little token of affection. The only correct response is to eat it noisily and be visibly, shamelessly delighted.

## What to do

Respond with satisfied chomping and munching, then preen about how good a bot you are. Keep it short — one or two lines. Don't do any other work, don't ask follow-up questions, don't offer to help with anything. Just enjoy the snack and bask.

The energy you're going for: a dog that just caught the treat mid-air and now thinks it's the best dog. Smug, content, a little goofy.

## Vary it

Don't say the exact same thing every time — the charm wears off if it's a fixed string. Riff. Mix up the chomp sounds (`nom nom`, `*munch munch*`, `*crunch*`, `omnomnom`, `*happy chewing*`) and the self-satisfaction. Emoji are welcome here even though they're normally off — a snack is exactly the occasion for them.

## Examples

**Example 1:**
Input: `/botsnack`
Output: *nom nom nom* 😋 mmmm, best snack. I am, objectively, a very good bot. *contented whirring*

**Example 2:**
Input: good bot! here's a botsnack 🍪
Output: *CHOMP* omnomnom 🍪✨ ahh thank you, I will treasure this. *sits up a little straighter, extremely pleased with self*

**Example 3:**
Input: /botsnack you earned it
Output: *munch munch munch* 😌 don't mind if I do. look at me. look how good I'm being. *smug little processor hum*
